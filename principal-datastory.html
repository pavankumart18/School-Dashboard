This Week's Lead Story
Admissions demand is holding, but conversion has slipped to 18.4%
Enquiries remain steady at 142 this week, yet conversion to enrolment declined by 2.1 points. Applications at 61 and offers at 44 signal that follow-up speed is the key bottleneck.

FTEs are stable at 1,148 (+2 vs last week), so volume is not the issue. The drop is in stage progression: enquiry to application and application to offer are both weaker than the four-week average.


Priority action is to recover response time during peak enquiry hours. A two-counselor shift to peak slots is projected to recover 3 to 5 additional enrolments per week.

"The enquiry funnel is healthy. The leak is timing - follow-up speed, not demand."

- Admissions Pulse, Week 26

Recommendation: tighten follow-up within 24 hours and trigger automated reminders after 48 hours of inactivity.
